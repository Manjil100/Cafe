Our Story

Born from obsession, built with purpose

Manjil's Cafe started in a 200-square-foot garage in the Mission District. Two friends, a second-hand Probat roaster, and an unreasonable belief that coffee could be something more — something personal, transparent, and unforgettable.

Today, we source directly from farms in Kathmandu, Nepal and New Zealand. Every bag is traceable to the lot. Every roast is profiled for the bean, not the trend.

Direct Trade

Sourced from farms we know by name, paying above fair-trade minimums.

Small Batch Roasted

15kg max per batch, profiled individually for peak expression.

Hand Crafted

Every drink made to order by skilled baristas, never automated.

Sustainable

Compostable packaging, zero-waste kitchen, carbon-offset delivery.

FAQ

Common questions

Do you take walk-ins or only reservations?
Both! Walk-ins are always welcome for casual seating. Reservations are recommended for weekends, groups of 4+, and our events. You can reserve right here on the website — it takes 30 seconds.
Do you have dairy-free and vegan options?
Absolutely. We offer house-made oat, almond, soy, and coconut milk at no extra charge. Most of our pastries have vegan alternatives — just ask the barista. Our full vegan pastry menu is marked with a leaf icon on the display case.
Can I buy your beans to brew at home?
Yes! We sell all our single-origin beans in 250g and 1kg bags, whole bean or ground to your specifications. We also offer a subscription service — choose your roast preference and frequency, and we'll deliver fresh to your door within 48 hours of roasting.
Is your space wheelchair accessible?
Yes. Our Valencia Street location is fully ADA accessible with a street-level entrance, accessible restroom, and adjustable-height tables. If you have any specific needs, don't hesitate to reach out and we'll make sure you're comfortable.
Do you offer Wi-Fi and power outlets?
We do — fast, free Wi-Fi and power outlets at every table. We're popular with remote workers, especially on weekday mornings. On weekends, we encourage a more social atmosphere, but you're still welcome to plug in.
Do you cater private events?
We do! We've hosted everything from product launches to intimate dinners to wedding after-parties. Our catering menu includes custom coffee bars, pastry spreads, and full brunch service. Get in touch at hello@emberandbrew.com and we'll build something special together.
